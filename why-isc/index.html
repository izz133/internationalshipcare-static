Why ISC

Why International Shipcare

Premium Layup Solutions

We provide customized preservation methods specific to each vessels, utilizing our well established and proven preservation techniques and procedures. Our team is comprised of senior mariners with extensive experience in the layup industry.

Secured Location

ISC’s layup locations are safe and secure, well sheltered from adverse weather and typhoon paths. All our layup locations are approved and gazetted by the Malaysian Government as an anchorage designated solely for the purpose of layup.

Experienced & Professional Personnel

Your vessel will be in the care of our team of technical experts, with strong industry knowledge, skills and qualification in various aspects of the Maritime industry.

Safety & Security

We provide watchkeeping services offering 24/7/365 communication to our land based communication centre. Our team performs ongoing inspection, audit and verification against our documented quality assurance and safety standards. ISC’s layup anchorages are continuously patrolled by Malaysian Maritime Enforcement Agency and the Malaysian Royal Navy.

Consistent Reporting System

Our clients receive dedicated weekly reports on the status of their laid-up vessel, and comprehensive monthly technical reports.

Quality by LRQA

We implement layup procedures developed from gap analysis of various class requirements. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified.

Established Business

With almost 50 years of unmatched operating experience, ISC has built a strong and productive association with all relevant Government Bodies in Malaysia.

Supply Chain

Our vendor selection process is rigorous and we can assure our clients that all third party services will fully meet their technical and quality requirements.

Cost

We will always focus on delivering the highest standard of service at a competitive cost, and have the technical capability to offer a host of additional value added services where required.
